About us

“Prime Travel” touristic agency was founded in 2016 and is situated on Tumanyan Street, right in the heart of Yerevan city. Being newly founded in a very competitive market, “Prime Travel” has quickly ained the trust and high praise of its customers due to its reliability and the vast variety of services provided.

By analysing the current market demand we have created a very flexible service package so that you can travel to any destination in the world at any moment without having to worry about anything. We have also acquired trustworthy partners abroad to insure the best quality travel experience for you.

Although nowadays the Armenian market is full of travel agencies, however we are more than assured that our professional customer service, personal approach in addition to the comprehensive range of services will satisfy even the toughest customer. “Prime Travel” has made it its mission to make your day to day international travelling arrangements as well as plan unforgettable vacations full of unique experiences to any corner of the world.
